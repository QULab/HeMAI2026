Welcome to **HeMAI 2026** — a workshop on **Multimodal Interaction with Generative AI Health Applications**.

HeMAI 2026 is organized in conjunction with the  
[28th ACM International Conference on Multimodal Interaction (ICMI 2026)](https://icmi.acm.org/2026/).

Generative AI and multimodal interaction open new opportunities for transparent, interactive, and user-centered health applications—while raising key challenges around privacy, safety, evaluation, and explainability.
